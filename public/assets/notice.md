# Important Notice #

The core advantage of Vercel lies in its serverless architecture and generous free tier, which make for an excellent user experience.
Once deployed, you can use it as a lightweight image host, or store static documents and generate shareable links.
However, keep these two limitations in mind:
First, Vercel is not a video hosting platform. Because it lacks streaming cache mechanisms, large videos will load extremely slowly. It's best to upload your videos to YouTube or Bilibili and embed them instead.
Second, since the servers are located overseas, avoid uploading excessively large images to prevent excessive bandwidth consumption and slow loading times.

"You can directly upload your images here, but make sure to compress them first. If you're still concerned about access speed and bandwidth consumption, you can use the Base64 encoding tool mentioned in our official `typace-official` repository. It's incredibly handy and allows you to convert any image into a pure text format. Of course, this will actually increase the file size a bit."
